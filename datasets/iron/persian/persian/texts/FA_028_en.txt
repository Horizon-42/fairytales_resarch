A traveler saves a talking serpent from villagers. At night she sheds her skin, revealing a maiden, and asks him to keep her secret. They marry quietly.

Jealous kin discover the serpent skin and burn it. The maiden is forced to return to the spring where she first appeared.

She leaves signs for her husband. He follows, bathes in the spring, and proves his loyalty. The serpent wife returns in human form, her curse broken, and they reunite.
