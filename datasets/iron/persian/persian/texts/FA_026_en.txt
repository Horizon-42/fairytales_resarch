A hunter in Mazandaran sees swan maidens bathing in a lake. He hides one feather cloak, stranding a maiden who begs for its return. He promises protection and marriage, and she agrees, asking him to keep the cloak hidden forever.

They wed and have a child. Jealous kin rummage through the house and find the cloak.
The swan maiden takes it back and must return to the sky. Before leaving, she leaves signs for the hunter if he wishes to follow.
He journeys to the sky realm, completes tasks set by her father, and wins permission. They reunite and return together.
